Install:

place the atom folder into your ZBRrush Misc Macros directory.

example: 

R8
C:\Program Files\Pixologic\ZBrush 4R8\ZStartup\Macros\Misc

R7
C:\Program Files (x86)\Pixologic\ZBrush 4R7\ZStartup\Macros\Misc

menu location:

you can find atom in the zplugin menu in zbrush
